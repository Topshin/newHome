What is FITT?

FITT is a healthcare data platform based on ICT exercise tests software. The current service is Saas B2B format that has exercise tests, Smart CRM, and Smart Scheduler provided to fitness centers and personal trainers. The users, fitness centers and trainers, are the distributors of the service and the collectors of exercise tests data from their customers. About 600 fitness centers have joined and about 100 fitness centers/trainers regularly pay for FITT service. FITT has accumulated about 40,000 users’ data in the data base since the service has launched (Feb 2016).

With ICT exercise tests software and its algorithms, FITT is positioning between healthcare/ fitness and medical-care. Our vision is providing metabolic disease prediction to as many people as we can to help people to have pre-preventive lifestyle in daily life.

Differentiating factors & competitive edge

FITT’s exercise tests software (Saas) is the only software that is based on indirect measurement and evaluation algorithms. Current healthcare devices and services are providing and showing information nothing related to a user’s health-state such as users’ running/exercise distance, speed, time, and calorie consumption, but FITT diagnose a user’s fitness level compared to sema age and gender group and provide personalized and optimized exercise programs for promoting his/her health-state in one-page reports by the three types of exercise tests[TMT, FMT, and SMT].
